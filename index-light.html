The Complete Protocol

12 Sessions. 4 Phases.
Your Protocol.

Each phase builds on the last — science-backed, teacher-led, peer-driven.

Phase 1 · Sessions 1–3 Foundation
Learning Goals
  • Understand aging as a modifiable trajectory, not a fixed decline
  • Articulate your personal healthspan goals with clarity and specificity
  • Shift mindset from fixing problems to designing resilient systems
90-Minute Flow

Welcome + framing → Mini-lecture on the biology of aging → Guided personal reflection → Breakout group introductions → Group synthesis and discussion → Introduction to the "Longevity Lens" framework

Between-Session Work

Complete a 3-day lifestyle awareness log tracking energy, meals, sleep, movement, and mood patterns.

Learning Goals
  • Understand glucose as a signal, not a villain — and why it matters for aging
  • See how metabolic dysfunction develops silently over years
  • Understand why weight alone is a poor marker of metabolic health
90-Minute Flow

Review lifestyle logs → Visual walkthrough of glucose curves → Case comparisons (healthy vs. dysregulated) → Myth dismantling session → CGM preview and introduction

Between-Session Work

Complete a detailed food recall and energy crash mapping exercise — identify when you crash, what you ate, and how you felt.

Learning Goals
  • Stop thinking in dietary rules; start thinking in metabolic responses
  • Understand how protein, fiber, and fat processing affects glucose
  • Design a default meal framework personalized to your biology
90-Minute Flow

Interactive lecture on nutrient responses → Meal deconstruction exercise → Group comparison of individual responses → Design your default meal framework

Between-Session Work

Try 1–2 meal adjustments based on your framework and document the results in your learning journal.

Phase 2 · Sessions 4–5 Data & Discovery
Learning Goals
  • Understand how CGM technology works and what it measures
  • Learn what the data actually means — spikes, variability, baselines
  • Reduce anxiety around numbers and develop a healthy data relationship
90-Minute Flow

CGM fundamentals lecture → Walk through real anonymized datasets → Group interpretation exercises → CGM setup guidance for those using devices

Between-Session Work

Begin your CGM experiment or review provided anonymized datasets. Record observations about your own patterns.

Learning Goals
  • Learn how eating sequence (food order) materially impacts glucose response
  • Understand carbohydrate context — why the same carb acts differently in different meals
  • Reduce glucose spikes without restriction or deprivation
90-Minute Flow

Share CGM observations from the week → Facilitator-guided curve comparison → Design safe food experiments → Expectation-setting for realistic progress

Between-Session Work

Run individual food experiments — try different meal orders, compositions, and timings. Document results with CGM data or subjective energy tracking.

Phase 3 · Sessions 6–8 Lifestyle Systems
Learning Goals
  • Understand muscle as the body's primary glucose sink
  • Learn the minimum effective dose of movement for metabolic benefit
  • Design a sustainable, age-appropriate movement plan
90-Minute Flow

CGM examples with and without post-meal movement → Movement planning workshop → Peer review of individual plans → Commitment check-in

Between-Session Work

Run movement experiments with CGM tracking — compare glucose curves on days with and without post-meal walks or resistance training.

Learning Goals
  • See sleep as a metabolic regulation system, not just rest
  • Understand the effects of late eating, blue light, and irregular schedules on glucose
  • Design one targeted sleep experiment based on your data
90-Minute Flow

Overnight CGM data review → Sleep habit audit → Troubleshooting workshop for common sleep disruptors → Design your personal sleep experiment

Between-Session Work

Run your sleep experiment for one week — track bedtime, wake time, late eating, screen exposure, and overnight glucose patterns.

Learning Goals
  • Recognize that emotions and stress cause measurable glucose spikes — without any food
  • Learn evidence-based nervous system regulation tools
  • Map your personal stress triggers and build a regulation plan
90-Minute Flow

CGM stress spike examples → Guided nervous system regulation exercise → Personal trigger mapping → Design your regulation toolkit

Between-Session Work

Practice your chosen stress regulation technique daily. Note any glucose changes during stressful events vs. calm periods.

Phase 4 · Sessions 9–12 Integration
Learning Goals
  • Avoid biohacking traps and expensive interventions with no evidence
  • Learn decision filters for evaluating supplements, tests, and wearable data
  • Develop critical thinking about health marketing claims
90-Minute Flow

Supplement label teardown exercise → Wearable data obsession discussion → Practice applying the decision framework to real products → Group debate

Between-Session Work

Conduct a personal supplement audit — evaluate everything you currently take against the decision framework. Cut what doesn't pass.

Learning Goals
  • Understand the psychosocial predictors of healthy aging — loneliness, purpose, community
  • Recognize that social connection is as impactful as diet and exercise
  • Design intentional social habits that support your healthspan
90-Minute Flow

Research review on social health and longevity → Values reflection exercise → Social habit design workshop → Accountability pairing

Between-Session Work

Complete a social connection inventory — map your relationships, identify gaps, and initiate one new or renewed connection this week.

Learning Goals
  • Integrate all 10 sessions into a coherent, sustainable personal plan
  • Build a realistic blueprint without overwhelm or overcommitment
  • Design your own failure recovery systems and habit stacks
90-Minute Flow

Blueprint construction workshop → Peer coaching sessions (paired review) → Failure planning and obstacle pre-emption → Draft sharing and feedback

Between-Session Work

Complete your Personal Longevity Blueprint — a comprehensive document covering nutrition, movement, sleep, stress, social health, and monitoring systems.

Learning Goals
  • Reflect on your transformation from session 1 to session 12
  • Synthesize your CGM insights and behavioral experiments into lasting habits
  • Build a concrete 90-day action plan for continued progress
90-Minute Flow

Before/after reflections → CGM insights synthesis → Commitment statements → 90-day action plan construction → Graduation and celebration

Final Output

Your completed Personal Longevity Blueprint + 90-Day Action Plan — two living documents that guide your healthspan journey beyond the course.

Why This Course
Is Different

Live, not recorded.

Every session is live with a certified instructor and your cohort. You ask questions in real time. You get real answers. Recordings are available if you miss one, but the live room is where the learning happens.

Your data, your curriculum.

You don't memorize facts about glucose or sleep — you watch them move in your own body. CGM readings, lifestyle logs, and food experiments shape every session you attend.

Small cohorts, real accountability.

Eight to fifteen people. You'll know your classmates' names and they'll know yours. Peer coaching in the final weeks builds the accountability that keeps the protocol alive.

A written protocol you'll keep.

The course ends with a document — your personal longevity blueprint, tested against your body over twelve weeks. It's the one deliverable designed to outlast the course itself.
