The Syllabus

Twelve sessions.
Four phases.

Each phase builds on the last, ending with a protocol you wrote and tested yourself.

01

Reframing Aging

Healthspan vs lifespan, compression of morbidity, your personal healthspan goals, and the Longevity Lens — metabolism, muscle, sleep, stress, and connection.

02

Metabolic Health

Blood sugar as the central signal of metabolic health. Glucose variability, flexibility, and a first look at CGM.

03

Nutrition for Longevity

Protein needs as you age, fiber, ultra-processed food, meal deconstruction, and the principles of a personalized plate.

04

CGM: Seeing the Invisible

How continuous glucose monitoring works, reading real datasets, and the observation checklist you'll use throughout the course.

05

Food Order & Glucose Curves

Fiber and protein first, fat's role, liquid vs solid carbs, and designing food experiments you can run at home.

06

Movement as a Glucose Tool

Muscle as a glucose sink, post-meal walking, resistance training fundamentals, and building a weekly movement plan.

07

Sleep & Circadian Rhythm

Circadian misalignment, cortisol rhythm, sleep debt, overnight glucose, and sleep experiments you can run this week.

08

Stress & Emotions

The cortisol–glucose loop, emotional glucose spikes, nervous system regulation, and mapping your personal triggers.

09

Supplements, Tests & Health Tech

An evidence hierarchy, HbA1c vs CGM, the supplements that actually matter, and a decision framework for new tools.

10

Social Health & Purpose

Psychosocial predictors of longevity, isolation and mortality, purpose and values reflection.

11

Personal Longevity Blueprint

Integration week. Peer coaching, draft protocol review, and planning for the moments you'll fall off track.

12

Reflection & the Next 90 Days

Before and after. Your CGM synthesis, your written protocol, and your commitment statements.

How It Works

From enrollment
to your own protocol.

Four phases. Twelve weeks. Each step builds on the last, ending with a protocol you wrote and tested yourself.

01

Apply & Match

Submit a short application. We review your background and goals, then place you in a cohort of 8–15 people with compatible interests and schedules.

Takes about 5 minutes
02

Baseline Assessment

Complete a health intake questionnaire and share any existing lab work. This becomes your personal starting point — the data you'll track against over twelve weeks.

Health data + lab review
03

Learn Live Weekly

Attend 90-minute live online sessions with your cohort and instructor. Each week covers a new longevity topic — with real data, case studies, and hands-on exercises.

12 weeks · 90 min each
04

Build Your Protocol

In the final phase, you assemble everything you've learned into a written, personalized longevity protocol — tested, peer-reviewed by your cohort, and yours to keep.

What Makes This Different

Built for practice,
not just theory.

Live, not recorded.

Every session is live with a certified instructor and your cohort. You ask questions in real time. You get real answers. Recordings are available if you miss one, but the live room is where the learning happens.

Your data, your curriculum.

You don't memorize facts about glucose or sleep — you watch them move in your own body. CGM readings, lifestyle logs, and food experiments shape every session you attend.

Small cohorts, real accountability.

Eight to fifteen people. You'll know your classmates' names and they'll know yours. Peer coaching in the final weeks builds the accountability that keeps the protocol alive.

A written protocol you'll keep.

The course ends with a document — your personal longevity blueprint, tested against your body over twelve weeks. It's the one deliverable designed to outlast the course itself.
